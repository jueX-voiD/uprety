Our Philosophy

Most trading education overcomplicates things. We don’t.
At Uprety EDU, we teach one thing well — how to read and trade alongside institutional money flow. No indicators stacked on indicators, no guessing, no gambling. Just understanding why price moves the way it does and positioning yourself on the right side of it.

We believe the market isn’t random. Every candle, every sweep, every breakout has intent behind it. Our job is to teach you how to see that intent clearly so you can stop reacting to the market and start anticipating it.
We also believe that real education isn’t passive. You don’t learn to trade by watching hundreds of hours of content and never placing a trade. That’s why our approach is built around live sessions, real charts, and real-time decision making. Theory matters, but execution is everything.

Consistency comes from clarity, and clarity comes from keeping it simple. We strip away the noise, give you a repeatable framework, and help you build the discipline to follow it — day after day, trade after trade.
This isn’t a shortcut. It’s a skill. And like any skill, it takes time, practice, and the right guidance. That’s what we’re here for.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your mentorship program about?

Our mentorship program is designed to guide new traders step-by-step through the fundamentals of trading. We focus on building a solid foundation, covering everything from basic terminology to advanced strategies—no prior experience required.

Who is this program for?

This program is ideal for complete beginners or those who’ve tried trading before but haven’t found consistent success. If you’re eager to learn trading the right way, from the ground up, this is for you.

What markets do you cover?

We primarily focus on the stock market, but also offer mentorship in options trading, depending on the student’s goals and experience level.

Do I need any prior experience to join?

Not at all. Our mentorship starts from the basics. Whether you’re a complete beginner or just looking to fill knowledge gaps, we meet you where you are.

What will I learn in the mentorship?

You’ll learn:

  • Trading terminology and platform setup
  • Technical analysis and chart reading
  • Risk management strategies
  • Trading psychology and discipline
  • Building a trading plan and routine
  • Live trade examples and analysis
How is the mentorship delivered?

We offer a mix of:

  • Live 1-on-1 sessions
  • Interactive Q&A and chart reviews
  • Community support (Discord, etc.)
