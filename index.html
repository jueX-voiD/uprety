Frequently Asked Questions

What is your mentorship program about?

Our mentorship program is designed to guide new traders step-by-step through the fundamentals of trading. We focus on building a solid foundation, covering everything from basic terminology to advanced strategies—no prior experience required.

Who is this program for?

This program is ideal for complete beginners or those who’ve tried trading before but haven’t found consistent success. If you’re eager to learn trading the right way, from the ground up, this is for you.

What markets do you cover?

We primarily focus on the stock market, but also offer mentorship in options trading, depending on the student’s goals and experience level.

Do I need any prior experience to join?

Not at all. Our mentorship starts from the basics. Whether you’re a complete beginner or just looking to fill knowledge gaps, we meet you where you are.

What will I learn in the mentorship?

You’ll learn:

  • Trading terminology and platform setup
  • Technical analysis and chart reading
  • Risk management strategies
  • Trading psychology and discipline
  • Building a trading plan and routine
  • Live trade examples and analysis
How is the mentorship delivered?

We offer a mix of:

  • Live 1-on-1 sessions
  • Interactive Q&A and chart reviews
  • Community support (Discord, etc.)
